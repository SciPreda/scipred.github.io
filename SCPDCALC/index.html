About This Calculator

SciPred's Calculator is an Experimental Tool for calculating the most sensible as well as the most absurd values. It ranges from calculating the discriminant of a Quadratic Equation to counting the number of leading zeros in a 32-bit binary number conversion.

It developed from a habit to overcomplicate things, which isn't that surprising really.

Unfortunately, full calculator accuracy cannot be guaranteed because of certain computational problems with handling decimal operations and functions, as well as the inability to handle considerately large numbers such as 1e400.
