STRINGTOOL

A work in progress by SciPred

Version 1.0

Stringtool is an experimental tool simply used to edit a piece of string.

You can edit the string as many times as you want.

Use the tools provided to edit the string in multiple ways.

You can slice part of the string, duplicate it, remove uppercase letters, reset it, reverse it, and so on.

To check the tools, simply hover your mouse somewhere at the top of this page, in an area surrounded by the slightly darker shade.

If you do not know the functions of a specific tool, hover over its icon and refer to the tooltip located at the bottom left.

When an error occurs, you will see an icon at the bottom right of the page. Click on the icon to see what the error was.
